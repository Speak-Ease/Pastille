Key ingredients behind the concept

The fictional formula is based on ingredients that have been studied for stress-related effects.

🍃

L-Theanine

A naturally occurring amino acid found in green tea and studied for possible relaxation and stress support.

🌱

Ashwagandha

An herbal extract studied for possible stress-relieving properties in healthy adults.

Research foundation

The evidence supports discussion of the individual ingredients—not proof that the fictional product itself works.

L-Theanine: Williams, Jonathan L., et al. “The Effects of L-Theanine Consumption on Stress and Anxiety in People Exposed to Stressful Conditions: A Systematic Review.” Journal of Herbal Medicine, vol. 29, 2021, article 100515.

Ashwagandha: Lopresti, Adrian L., et al. “A Randomized, Double-Blind, Placebo-Controlled Study of Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) on Stress and Anxiety in Healthy Adults.” Medicine, vol. 98, no. 37, 2019.

Frequently asked questions

Is Speak Ease Pastille a real product?

No. It is a fictional product created for a Georgia Highlands College persuasive speech assignment.

Can it be purchased?

No. The website is a visual aid and product concept only.

Does this website provide medical advice?

No. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent anxiety. Students experiencing severe anxiety should seek guidance from a qualified health professional or campus support service.

Why include research?

The cited studies provide a scientific foundation for discussing L-theanine and ashwagandha while avoiding unsupported claims about the fictional formula.
